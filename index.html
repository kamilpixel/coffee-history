The Fascinating Journey ofCoffeeThrough Time

Coffee is more than just a drink — it's a global culture that connects people from all walks of life. From its legendary discovery in Ethiopia to modern specialty cafes, coffee's story is rich with myth, trade, and innovation

The Origins - Ethiopia (9th Century)

According to legend, coffee was discovered by an Ethiopian goat herder named Kaldi, who noticed his goats becoming energetic after eating red berries from a shrub. Monks later used these berries to stay awake during long prayers — marking the first recorded use of coffee as a stimulant
